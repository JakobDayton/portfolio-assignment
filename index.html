About Me

Hi! My name's Jakob Dayton and I'm a queer music producer, graphic designer, costume maker, novice web developer, and all around nerd from Charlotte, North Carolina. Not content being a one trick pony, I've added a number of metaphorical feathers to my metaphorical cap in hopes of finding a career where I can thrive off of my passions. I'm an avid people pleaser and work well in groups as well as on my own. I excel in combining analytical thinking and creativity in order to make my ideas come alive. With experience in audio software such as Ableton Live and Serum, visual design programs like Adobe Photoshop and Aseprite, as well as coding experience in Visual Studio, I might just be the right fit for you or your team.
